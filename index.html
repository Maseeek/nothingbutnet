What do we do?

Nothing But Net will help you become the shooter that you aspire to be. By using analytics determined from your video, we can give you personal shooting tips, track your field goal percentage and determine your optimal release angle.

Simply upload a video of you shooting a basketball, and we will do the rest. Our software will analyze your shot and give you feedback on how to improve. We will also track your field goal percentage and give you tips on how to improve it.

Nothing But Net is the best choice for any basketball player looking to improve their shooting. Our software is easy to use and will give you the feedback you need to become a better shooter. We are dedicated to helping you reach your full potential on the court.
